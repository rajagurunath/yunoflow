My recommendation

B · Vault for the public landing/pricing (light + serif reads most “enterprise / finance-grade” and differentiates from every dark AI-tool clone), paired with A · Midnight Ledger for the console (operators live in a focused dark workspace). It’s the classic premium-SaaS move: editorial light marketing, instrument-grade dark product. C · Control Room is the boldest single-aesthetic play if you want the whole thing to scream “engineering.”
